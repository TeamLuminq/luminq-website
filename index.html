Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Luminq

What is Luminq?

Luminq is the first full-service LinkedIn video format studio. We help business leaders grow their visibility, trust, influence and authority on LinkedIn through human-led video content and formats, strategic content planning, and full production and management services. We are strategic first, creative second, and always outcome-led.

Why should leaders use video on LinkedIn?

Video on LinkedIn gets 5x more engagement than other content types and is shared 20x more. Human faces on video foster trust — 71% of buyers are more likely to purchase when a CEO is active on social media. LinkedIn has 1.2 billion users with video views up 36% year-on-year, and 82% of buyers view profiles before making purchasing decisions. Video formats give leaders a repeatable framework to deliver confidently while keeping viewers hooked through short-form storytelling.

What are video formats?

Video formats are structured, repeatable and scalable frameworks that provide a way to show your best side on camera. They give leaders a consistent structure that builds confidence and creates consistency.

We select a delivery format depending on how leaders naturally explain complex ideas. This could be in an interview, vodcast, behind the scenes, round table, quiz, lecture, exploring nature, or adventure format — to give some examples.

Formats provide structure. Structure builds confidence. Confidence creates consistency.

How much time do I need to commit?

Luminq is designed for minimal time commitment from busy leaders. At the Visibility level, the commitment is just 3–4 hours per month — typically a single filming session. We handle everything else: strategy, format and story development, scripting, editing, publishing, commenting, optimisation and page management.

What makes Luminq different from a ghostwriter or social media agency?

Unlike ghostwriters, we capture your real voice on video — your face, your words, your energy. Unlike generic agencies, we combine broadcast-quality video production with platform-native strategy specifically for LinkedIn. We focus on commercial outcomes, not vanity metrics. Every piece of content connects back to your business goals. And our format-led approach gives you a repeatable framework to deliver confidently while keeping viewers hooked through short-form storytelling.

What does the process look like?

We follow a four-stage process: (1) Diagnose — we audit your LinkedIn presence, identify competitors, and find quick wins. (2) Define — we identify your content pillars and video formats, tone of voice, and credibility angles. (3) Delivery Plan — we create an actionable content roadmap and publishing schedule. (4) Execute & Optimise — we handle filming, editing, publishing, engagement and ongoing optimisation with regular analytics reporting.
