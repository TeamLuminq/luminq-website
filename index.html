What are video formats?

Video formats are structured, repeatable and scalable frameworks that provide a way to show your best side on camera. They give leaders a consistent structure that builds confidence and creates consistency.

We select a delivery format depending on how leaders naturally explain complex ideas. This could be in an interview, vodcast, behind the scenes, round table, quiz, lecture, exploring nature, or adventure format — to give some examples.

Formats provide structure. Structure builds confidence. Confidence creates consistency.

What is LinkedIn personal branding?

LinkedIn personal branding is the deliberate process of building a consistent, visible professional identity on LinkedIn - one that establishes your expertise, earns trust with your target audience, and creates commercial opportunities over time. For senior leaders and executives, it means going beyond a polished profile to actively publishing content that positions you as a recognisable authority in your field. At Luminq, we take this further: rather than generic content, we build video-led LinkedIn formats and strategies rooted in your genuine expertise, so your personal brand video drives real business outcomes, not just follower counts.

How does LinkedIn video help B2B sales?

LinkedIn video works in B2B sales because it does what written content can't: it shows the human behind the business. When a potential client watches your CEO or senior leader speak with clarity and conviction about a topic they care about, trust builds faster than any case study or cold email ever could. LinkedIn video gets five times the engagement of text posts and is shared twenty times more - meaning your message reaches decision makers, not just your existing connections. At Luminq, every video strategy we build is designed to move prospects along a clear path from awareness to commercial action.
