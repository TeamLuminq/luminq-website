Privacy Notice

Luminq is a trading name of The Connected Set Limited.

This Privacy Notice explains what personal data (information) we hold about you, how we collect it, and how we will use and may share information about you when you use our services, visit our website, or engage with us.

This Privacy Notice applies to clients, prospective clients, website visitors, individuals who submit enquiries through our contact forms, and contributors to our video content productions.

1. Who collects information about you?

The Connected Set Limited, trading as Luminq ("Luminq", "we", "us"), is a 'data controller'. This means that we are responsible for deciding how we hold and use certain information about you. The Connected Set Limited is registered in England and Wales.

2. What information do we collect about you?

We may collect the following information about you:

  • Contact information: Your name, email address, phone number, and business address as provided through our contact forms, email correspondence, or during the course of our working relationship.
  • Professional information: Your job title, company name, LinkedIn profile URL, and role as provided when you enquire about our services.
  • Content contribution information: If you participate in video content production with us, we may collect your image, voice, and other information contained in your contribution that identifies you.
  • Website usage information: Technical data about your visits to our website, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Payment information: If we invoice you for services, we may collect billing details necessary to process payment.

3. How do we use your information?

We process your information for the purposes of our business, including for content production, service delivery, and marketing. As a 'data controller', we are required to have a legal basis for processing your personal data. We will use the information we collect about you for:

A) The performance of our contract with you.
This includes processing your information in connection with providing our LinkedIn video content and format services, including strategy, production, publishing, and management services.

B) Responding to your enquiries.
When you submit an enquiry through our website or contact us by email, we process your contact information to respond to your request and to discuss how we may be able to help you.
Legal basis: Necessary for our legitimate interests to manage and administer our business effectively.

C) Content production and distribution.
If you participate in video production with us, we process information connected with your contribution, such as video footage, images, and audio recordings. This content may be published on LinkedIn and other platforms as agreed with you.
Legal basis: Performance of our contract with you and our legitimate interests in delivering our services.

D) Marketing and communications.
We may contact you about our services, industry insights, or opportunities to work together. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
Legal basis: Consent or our legitimate interests in promoting our services, where our interests do not override your rights and freedoms.

E) Website analytics.
We use analytics to understand how visitors use our website so that we can improve it.
Legal basis: Necessary for our legitimate interests in improving our website and services.

4. Who will we share your information with?

We will only share your information with third parties where disclosure is consistent with a legal basis for processing and where this is lawful and fair to you. Specific circumstances may include:

  • Sharing content (such as video footage published on LinkedIn) as agreed with you as part of our services.
  • Sharing information with service providers who assist us in delivering our services (such as video hosting platforms, analytics providers, or payment processors).
  • Disclosure required by law or to comply with a legal obligation.

5. Transfers of information outside the UK

In connection with our business, we may transfer your information outside the UK to data processors and service providers. Whenever we transfer your information outside the UK, we ensure a similar degree of protection is afforded to it by implementing appropriate safeguards. Please contact us if you would like further information about the specific mechanisms used.

6. Keeping your personal information secure

We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your information from being accidentally lost, used or accessed in an unauthorised way, altered or disclosed. We limit access to your information to those individuals who have a business need to know. We have also put in place procedures to deal with any suspected data security breach and will notify you and any applicable regulator where we are legally required to do so.

7. How long will we keep your information?

We will keep the information we obtain about you for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. For client records, we will typically retain information for the duration of our working relationship and for a reasonable period afterwards. Video content produced as part of our services may be retained for as long as it remains relevant to your LinkedIn presence, or as otherwise agreed.

8. Your rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information (a "data subject access request").
  • Request correction of any incomplete or inaccurate information we hold about you.
  • Request erasure of your personal information where there is no good reason for us continuing to process it.
  • Object to processing of your personal information where we are relying on a legitimate interest.
  • Request restriction of processing of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we are relying on consent to process your personal data.
